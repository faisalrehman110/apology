My apology

I am sorry, Alishba.

I was wrong in my approach. Completely. I asked for love, but my own attitude was not loving enough in that moment.

I asked for softness, but my tone was not soft. I asked to feel wanted, but I forgot that you also deserve to feel safe, heard, and gently held by my words.

I do not want to win an argument. I want to become better for the love I keep asking from you.

I know she is asking for love from me too. I know I am asking the same from her. But my way of asking was wrong, and I accept that without excuses.
