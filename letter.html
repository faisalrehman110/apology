My Alishba

You have a softness that stays in my heart even when the day becomes heavy. From the first quiet memory of a corridor to the conversations that became part of my routine, you became more than a feeling. You became a prayer I carry with care.

I admire your innocence, your moods, your shyness, your little reactions, and even the stubbornness that somehow still feels precious when it belongs to you. You turn simple things into memories, a word, a reply, a small joke, a quiet moment, a photograph, a prayer.

I am not asking you to forget what hurt you. I am asking for the chance to become gentler, wiser, and more deserving of the love I ask from you. If I ask for affection, I must give affection first. If I ask for softness, I must speak with softness first.

You are precious to me in a way I should protect with patience, not damage with anger. I choose you with respect, with care, and with the sincere dua that Allah keeps our hearts clean, loyal, and close.

- Your Faisal

Private. Respectful. Written to apologize, not to pressure.
