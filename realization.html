Timeline of realization

What I understand now

I was not wrong for needing love. I was wrong in the way I asked for it.

1

What happened

I asked for affection in the wrong tone.

I kept saying that I feel a lack of love and that I want to be spoken to with tenderness, with softness, care, and warm words. But I said it with anger instead of tenderness.

2

How it may have hurt you

You may have felt pressured instead of loved.

Maybe it felt like I was demanding love instead of giving it first. Maybe my words made your heart tired when your heart was also asking for softness from me.

3

What I should have done

I should have spoken with the same softness I wanted.

I should have held my tone, listened first, and expressed my need with care. Love should never sound like a complaint thrown in anger.

4

What I understand now

Love cannot feel safe when the tone feels unsafe.

If I want a loving voice, I must bring a loving voice. If I want affection, I must protect the space where affection can breathe.

5

What I will do now

I will change the way I ask for love.

I will not turn my hurt into anger. I will ask gently, listen carefully, and remember that your heart deserves the same love I keep asking from you.
